Frequently Asked Questions

User

Using the App

How do I log my blood glucose?

Click on the "Blood Glucose Log" in the sidebar, fill in the date, time, level, and any notes, then hit submit. The data is saved automatically.

Can I edit my past logs?

Editing isn't enabled yet — but you can delete logs manually by clearing browser storage or we may add an edit button soon.

Data & Privacy

Is my data secure?

Yes. All your data is stored locally in your browser only. It is not shared, uploaded, or accessible by others unless you allow it.

Does this site follow HIPAA rules?

While this isn't a certified medical tool, we follow privacy principles in line with HIPAA — your data belongs to you and is never used without consent.

Tracking Questions

What if I forget to log a meal or insulin dose?

That’s okay! Just log it later — the tracker allows you to select custom dates and times so you can backfill any entries.

Can I track Type 1 diabetes with this?

Yes — while designed with Type 2 in mind, anyone managing blood glucose, insulin, meals, or exercise can use this tool.

Troubleshooting

Why can't I see my dashboard or logs?

Make sure all your HTML files are saved in the same folder and the links match exactly (case sensitive!). Check your browser’s localStorage too.

Where do I report a bug?

You can use the "Report a Problem" button under the Dashboard Insights section to describe what’s wrong — we appreciate it!

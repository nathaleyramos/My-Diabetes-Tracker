Facts About Diabetes

User

1. Diabetes is a chronic condition — not a temporary one.

Once diagnosed, diabetes doesn’t go away. But with good management, you can live a long and healthy life.

2. Type 2 diabetes can often be prevented or delayed.

With healthy eating, physical activity, and regular check-ups, many people reduce their risk significantly.

3. Insulin isn’t just for Type 1.

Many people with Type 2 diabetes also need insulin, especially if lifestyle and oral meds aren't enough.

4. Uncontrolled diabetes leads to serious complications.

It can affect your eyes, kidneys, heart, and nerves. But with proper tracking and care, risks can be lowered.

5. You’re not alone.

Over 400 million people worldwide are living with diabetes — and support, tools, and resources are growing every year.
